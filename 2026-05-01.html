France ACRE Reform: Social Contribution Exemption Drops to 25% from July

Important

For micro-enterprises created from July 1, 2026 onwards in France, the ACRE social contribution exemption drops from 50% to 25%. Why it matters: If you or anyone on your team is considering launching a micro-enterprise in France, do it before July 1 to lock in the 50% ACRE rate. The halved exemption significantly impacts first-year cash flow for freelance developers and designers.
